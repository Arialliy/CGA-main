# CGA-v2 Paper Notes

Safe title:

> Component-Geometry Regularization for Target-Preserving Infrared Small Target Detection

Safe abstract sentence:

> CGA-v2 regularizes MSHNet with component-level geometric targets while preserving the final mask inference path; fixed-seed experiments on NUDT-SIRST show positive Full and mean HC-Val performance, while ablation analysis indicates mixed attribution rather than strict necessity of each auxiliary target.

Required caveat:

> Do not claim every seed reduces HC-Val FA. Seed44 HC-Val improves mIoU and Pd but worsens Precision and FA.
